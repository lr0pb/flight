11.20 November update notes

So, "Hello World"! I translated site into English. And what is the Flight? This is new photo view mode in which you travel by picturesque valley and move to its main points via fly effect.
A bit of history: I took off all photos in the spring 2019 and started to develop site, June 1 I opened beta, but it was still raw. Only August 1 I released first stable version. September 30 was an optimization update and finally today we can see the monumental work!
It's good, but what is the goal of the Flight? Via Flight I will share good vibes of warm spring period of flowering trees and also my photos :) Explore the world and discover new places!
