11.20 November update notes

So, "Hello World"! I translated site into English. And, what is Flight? This is new photo view mode, in which you travel by picturesque valley and move to its main points via fly effect.
A bit of history: I took off all photos in the spring 2019 and started to develop site, June 1 I opened beta, but it was still raw. Only August 1 I made release. September 30 was an optimization update, and finally today we can see the monumental work!
It's good, but what is the goal of Flight? Via Flight I will share good vibes of warm spring period of fllowering trees and also my pictures :) Explore the world and discover new places!

If you have any questions, write me!
